About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan. I moved down to Orlando in 2013 to attend school. I received my A.A. in Computer Science from Valencia College in May 2017. I am currently pursuing my B.A. in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida.

I first discovered my passion for writing code when taking an Introduction to Programming Concepts course at at Valencia. I transferred from a private college in up-state New York the year before; having changed my major twice (Exercise Science and Biochemistry). Before, I was unsure what career path I would choose and was starting to become discouraged. At long last I finally found something I enjoyed! I enjoy the logic-oriented nature of coding as well as being able to create something that solves a problem, does something cool, or both!
